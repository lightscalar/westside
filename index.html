Introduction

Welcome to Westside, a tough, albeit virtual, neighborhood. Its brutish inhabitants are agents who behave according to simple rules, but who can learn from experience. We'll discuss the details below, but here is the neighborhood's salient fact:

The inhabitants of Westside divide into two gangs: the Sharks & the Jets. The gangs are identical in most respects, but Sharks are blue, and Jets are orange. And for this reason, of course, Sharks and Jets are mortal enemies.

Movement

Gang members exhibit flocking behavior, as demonstrated by the simulation at the right. Agents instinctively seek friendly gang members and avoid enemies. They move in the same direction as the members of their local gang, and tend to move toward the center of the gang. But they also have a sense of personal space — they will not get too close to their fellow gang members.

The smaller the gang, the faster it moves — large gangs move at half the speed of individuals. But being in a large group does confer certain advantages in the dangerous world of Westside.
